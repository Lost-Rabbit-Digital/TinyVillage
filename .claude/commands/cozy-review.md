description: Review code/features against cozy game design principles
---

Review the recent changes or specified code against Tiny Village's cozy game design principles:

1. **Core Pillars Check:**
   - **Cozy & Accessible:** Is it low-stress? No fail states? Intuitive?
   - **Creative Expression:** Does it enhance player creativity?
   - **Meaningful Progression:** Rewarding without grinding?
   - **Simple Depth:** Easy to learn, engaging to master?

2. **Accessibility:**
   - Can it be understood without lengthy tutorials?
   - Are controls intuitive?
   - Is visual feedback clear?
   - Any colorblind considerations needed?
   - Text readable and appropriately sized?

3. **Cozy Aesthetic:**
   - Are colors warm and inviting (not harsh)?
   - Are sounds gentle (not jarring)?
   - Is the UI clean and uncluttered?
   - Does it feel relaxing to use?

4. **No Anti-Patterns:**
   - ❌ Time pressure or stress mechanics
   - ❌ Punishing failure states
   - ❌ Forced grinding or tedium
   - ❌ Pay-to-win elements
   - ❌ Dark patterns or FOMO mechanics
   - ❌ Harsh sounds or flashing lights

5. **Monetization Ethics (if applicable):**
   - Is it purely cosmetic or content expansion?
   - Is it optional for core experience?
   - Is pricing transparent and fair?
   - Are there free alternatives?
   - No loot boxes or predatory mechanics?

6. **Recommendations:**
   - List specific improvements to enhance coziness
   - Suggest accessibility enhancements
   - Propose UI/UX refinements
   - Identify any stress-inducing elements to remove

Provide a summary with a "Coziness Score" (1-10) and actionable suggestions.
